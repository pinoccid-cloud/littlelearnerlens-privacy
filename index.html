Privacy Policy - Little Learner Lens

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Little Learner Lens does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. Camera images are processed in real time and are not stored or shared.

Camera Permission

The app requires camera access to photograph objects. No photos are saved or transmitted.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google may collect device information to serve ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for parents to use with young children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Camera and Photo Data

When you photograph an object, that image is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a child-friendly explanation. The image is processed ephemerally — it is used only in the moment to generate the response and is not stored, retained or used for any other purpose by us or Anthropic. No photos are saved to your device by this app.

Device Identifiers

Google AdMob, our advertising partner, collects your device's advertising identifier (a unique ID assigned to your device) to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. This identifier is shared with Google's advertising network. You can reset or limit this identifier in your Android device settings under Google > Ads.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data ourselves. Any data processed by third party services (Google AdMob, Anthropic Claude API) is subject to their own retention policies. Any retained data is automatically deleted within 90 days.
